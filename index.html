Significance of English

English serves as a crucial link language in India, connecting people from various linguistic backgrounds. It is the language of business, government, and higher education. English has become a symbol of opportunity and modernity in Indian society.

Origin in India

The introduction of English to India began with the arrival of the British East India Company in the 17th century. It became more prominent with Lord Macaulay's education reforms in the 19th century, promoting English as the medium of instruction.
